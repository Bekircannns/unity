# UGC Plan (First 14 Days)

## Core creative rule
Show the dirty object and clean result in the first 2 seconds.

## Content formats
1. Before/after split
2. ASMR close-up
3. Fail -> fix
4. Speedrun timer
5. Can you spot it?
6. Tool comparison
7. One-tool challenge
8. Top 5 montage
9. Daily 5-minute routine
10. Comment decision prompt

## Week 1 objective: hook research
- Day 1-2: Before/after split (5 videos)
- Day 3-4: ASMR close-up (4 videos)
- Day 5: Fail -> fix (2 videos)
- Day 6: Speedrun (2 videos)
- Day 7: Can you spot it? (2 videos)

## Week 2 objective: scale winning formats
- Day 8-9: Tool comparison (3 videos)
- Day 10: One-tool challenge (2 videos)
- Day 11: Top 5 montage (2 videos)
- Day 12: Daily routine (2 videos)
- Day 13-14: Re-shoot top 2 formats (6 videos)

## Metrics to track daily
- Hook rate (first 2-3 seconds)
- Hold/watch time
- CTR
- CVR (click to install)
- IPM
- tutorial_complete
- first_3_levels_complete
- next_day_open

## Ethical guardrails
- No fake AI personas or fake influencer identities
- No misleading footage that does not exist in game
- Clear sponsor/partner disclosure when required
